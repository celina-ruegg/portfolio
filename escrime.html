ESCRIME DE LION

Fencing combines precision with elegance – an art that unites movement, concentration, and style. Inspired by this aesthetic, the fictional fencing school “Escrime de Lion” was created, whose visual identity captures the dynamism and excitement of the sport. The logo, typography, and motto reflect the lightness and energy of fencing. In this way, athletic movement becomes a visual language – a composition in which discipline, elegance, and expression merge.
