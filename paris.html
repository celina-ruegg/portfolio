ABOUT ME

PERSPECTIVES

A walk through Paris can be experienced in many different ways – depending on one’s perspective. This book accompanies you on a journey from the Cour Carrée to the Louvre and shows the city through two contrasting sets of eyes: the calm, objective observations of a father and the playful, colorful perceptions of his young daughter. Small doll illustrations lend the book a humorous lightness. This makes the shift in perspective not only visible but also tangible – a lively dialogue between seriousness and imagination.
